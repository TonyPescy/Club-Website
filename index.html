CatPuccino Club

We are a club that invites all cat lovers to come join us for coffee at The Purrfect Fix Cat Cafe! We welcome anyone and encourage them to bring their own cats with them. On the first meeting of every month we help the local shelters by allowing them to bring their cats to the club to hopefully find a new companion! We are all drawn together by our love for cats and coffee!
